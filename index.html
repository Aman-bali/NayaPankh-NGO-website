Our Story

Born from student passion,
driven by purpose

We started as a group of college students who refused to look away. Today, we are a registered NGO touching thousands of lives across Uttar Pradesh.

How It Began

From a dorm room to districts across UP

NayePankh Foundation was born out of conversations among university students who kept asking: why do some children never get a fair chance? In 2020, a small group decided to stop asking and start doing.

What began as weekend tutoring sessions in Kanpur's government school corridors grew into a full-fledged organization running programs across healthcare, education, and essential needs.

Today, NayePankh is officially registered with the Uttar Pradesh Government, holds 80G and 12A tax exemptions, and operates from two bases — Kanpur and Ghaziabad — with a growing volunteer network spanning 18 districts.

2020
Founded by students of Kanpur with a single tutoring session for 12 children. That first Saturday changed everything.

Our Work

Programs that create
lasting change

Designed by students, delivered on the ground — our programs address the most urgent gaps in education, health, and essential needs.

📖

Weekend Tutoring Program

Every weekend, trained student volunteers conduct free tutoring sessions for children in classes 3–10 at government schools in Kanpur and Ghaziabad. Focus areas include Math, Science, Hindi, and English.

500+ students12 schoolsKanpur & Ghaziabad
💻

Digital Literacy Initiative

Bridging the digital divide by teaching basic computer skills, internet safety, and government portal navigation to both children and adults in rural communities. Laptops and tablets are provided on loan.

200+ trainedComputer labsRural UP
🎒

School Supply Drive

Annual campaigns to collect and distribute school bags, stationery, uniforms, and books to children from BPL families before the academic year begins.

1,000+ kitsAnnualMultiple districts
🩺

Mobile Health Camps

Quarterly health camps in rural areas with qualified doctors and paramedics offering free consultations, blood pressure, sugar, and haemoglobin checks, and medicine distribution.

30+ camps3,000+ beneficiariesQuarterly
🧼

Swachh Sehat Abhiyan

Hygiene and sanitation awareness drives in schools and anganwadis covering handwashing, menstrual hygiene, clean water, and open-defecation-free practices.

50+ sessionsSchools & AWCs
🍼

Mother & Child Nutrition Drive

Monthly nutrition kits and awareness sessions for pregnant and lactating mothers in collaboration with local ASHA workers and anganwadis.

200+ mothersMonthly
🍱

Raahat Food Drive

Emergency and festive food distribution drives targeting migrant families, daily-wage workers, and homeless individuals especially during harsh winters and crisis periods.

5,000+ meals servedYear-round
🧥

Winter Clothing Campaign

Annual blanket and warm clothes collection and distribution before the winter season for underprivileged families in Kanpur and surrounding areas.

1,500+ itemsAnnual
👩‍🎓

Shakti Scholarships

Merit-cum-need scholarships for girls from BPL families to complete higher education. Each recipient is also mentored by a woman volunteer throughout the year.

50 scholarsAnnual
🌸

Pad Seva

Free distribution of biodegradable sanitary pads to adolescent girls in government schools along with menstrual hygiene education to break stigma and reduce school dropout rates.

800+ girls reachedMonthly
